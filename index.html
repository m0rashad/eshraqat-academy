About the Academy

About EshraQat Academy

EshraQat Academy is an Islamic educational academy specializing in youth development and Islamic learning. Our mission is to empower young Muslims with the essential knowledge and understanding needed to practice their religion with confidence, clarity, and purpose.

Through engaging programs, interactive activities, and a storytelling-based teaching approach, we inspire curiosity, strengthen identity, and nurture a genuine love for learning within our youth communities.

EshraQat Academy offers a variety of programs and initiatives, including:

  • Islamic history programs that revive forgotten stories and lessons from our rich heritage
  • Seerah programs focused on the life and character of Prophet Muhammad ﷺ
  • "Pearls from the Sahaba" programs highlighting inspiring stories from the companions of the Prophet ﷺ
  • Our successful "SABIQAT" intensive Islamic studies program for girls

At the heart of all our programs is a commitment to building strong Islamic character, connecting students with meaningful role models, and emphasizing the individual responsibility each member of the Muslim community carries in preserving and contributing positively to the Ummah.

At Eshraqat Academy, we strive to nurture a generation of confident, knowledgeable, and principled young Muslims who are prepared to lead the future of this Ummah with faith, purpose, and confidence.

Our Vision

Preparing the Mothers of Tomorrow's Leaders.

نُعِدُّ أمهات قادة الغد.

At the heart of Eshraqat Academy is a vision to empower Muslim girls and youth with the essential foundations they need to build confidence and live positively within their communities. We are deeply committed to preparing future mothers who will play a vital role in raising the next generation of leaders of this Ummah — by teaching the fundamentals of the religion across its key branches: Quran, Aqeedah, Hadith, Fiqh, Seerah, and Manners.

في صميم إشراقات أكاديمي رؤية تسعى إلى تمكين الفتيات المسلمات من الأسس الضرورية التي يحتجن إليها لبناء الثقة بالنفس والعيش بإيجابية داخل مجتمعاتهن. نحن ملتزمون التزاماً عميقاً بإعداد أمهات المستقبل اللواتي سيؤدين دوراً محورياً في تنشئة الجيل القادم من قادة هذه الأمة — وذلك من خلال تعليم أصول الدين في أبرز فروعه: القرآن الكريم، والعقيدة، والحديث الشريف، والفقه، والسيرة النبوية، والآداب الإسلامية.

About the Founder

A Vision Rooted in Knowledge & Service.

رؤيةٌ متجذِّرةٌ في العلم والخدمة.

Al-Azhar University graduate and a dedicated member of the Northern Virginia Muslim community, the founder of EshraQat Academy established the academy in 2024 with a passion for youth education, Islamic learning, and community service.

After studying at Al-Azhar, she moved to the United States and earned a Bachelor's degree in Electrical Engineering from George Mason University in 2009. Alongside her professional and academic journey, she remained deeply committed to teaching youth and serving the Muslim community. She is currently pursuing a Master's degree in Islamic Leadership at Boston Islamic Seminary.

Our Team

Flagship Program

SABIQAT

The Islamic Studies Program for Muslim Girls

برنامج الدراسات الإسلامية للفتيات المسلمات

SABIQAT is Eshraqat Academy's flagship program — a uniquely designed Islamic studies curriculum for Muslim girls ages 10–16. Over two structured semesters, students explore six core Islamic disciplines in an environment that is warm, engaging, and sisterhood-focused. More than a class — it's a journey. Students don't just learn; they reflect, connect, and grow.

سابقات هو البرنامج الرئيسي لإشراقات أكاديمي — منهج إسلامي مصمم خصيصاً لفتيات مسلمات من سن 10 إلى 16. على مدى فصلين دراسيين، تستكشف الطالبات ستة محاور إسلامية أساسية في بيئة دافئة ومحفزة تقوم على روح الأخوة. أكثر من مجرد درس — إنها رحلة. الطالبات لا يتعلمن فحسب، بل يتأملن ويتواصلن وينمون.
